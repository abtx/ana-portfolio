Modern banking appt

  • ux design
  • ui design
  • motion design
  • prototyping and user testing
Timeline
6 weeks
Software
Sketch, Photoshop, After Effects, Principle

Objective

To enhance the user experience by designing clear and user-friendly interface and user interaction features. Embracing transparency and functionality of the app, my proposition promotes financial responsibility and independence.

Visual hierarchy

  • Organised view that uses an interactive timeline helps to simplify and present essential information in a way that is receptive of distinct needs and end-goals of users.
  • Visual representation of data using graphs and bars helps users in a quick and effective identification of ratio between their income and outcome.

Function: The Overview function helps users in quick access to a summary of users income and outcome. Users can examine their spending across time.

User Oriented

  • Dynamic and responsive notifications allow users to efficiently move across the app to address a problematic area.
  • Directing users attention by implementing features that expand and highlight certain areas to help in guiding users through completing an action.

Function: Money Boxes function allows users to set personal or shared financial goals and collect money overtime by contributing directly to a money box or by scheduling a payment.

Responsive

  • Collapsing and expanding menu allows for a quick access to a selection of features from any point in the app.
  • Responsive and accessible money and account management helps in allowing users to control their accounts on demand.

Function: Drop Down Menu allows users to quickly overview their accounts, access and transfer funds between current and savings accounts and manage their account on the go.

Transparent

  • A clear and accessible overview of money flow through a list of other contributors and their contributions.
  • Simplified, visual elements such as colour coded bars or collection deadlines help users in a more effective understanding of their financial goals.

Function: Money Boxes function allows users to set personal or shared financial goals and collect money overtime by contributing directly to a money box or by scheduling a payment.

Engaging

  • Promoting meaning-making attitude by presenting users with visualisations of their goals by using photos and graphs.
  • Encouraging user participation and fostering the sense of community by enabling users to interact and follow actions of other contributors to grow excitement around their shared financial goal.

Function: Shared Money Boxes include a comment section with the most recent comments from collection participants.

Organised

  • Revealing information gradually declutters screen and provides fully customisable view of data presented to users.
  • Targeting and highlighting specific positions on the graph by assigning coloured tags and dividing spendings by categories allow users an access to a clear and organised view.

Function: My Spending Habits feature helps users to understand and learn about nature of their finances.

Objective

To enhance the user experience by designing clear and user-friendly interface and user interaction features. Embracing transparency and functionality of the app, my proposition promotes financial responsibility and independence.

Mimo.hue is a personal project of mine for which I create experimental and abstract artworks. The idea behind Mimo.hue is to ‘Let the unexpected happen.’, which is reflected in aesthetics of my artwork (flowing shapes, colourful gradients and handmade textures).

For this prototype, I have explored the idea of allowing users to experience and appreciate the art more by avoiding using too many ui elements at once. I have achieved that by dividing the flow into stages that make the interface expand and change gradually as a consequence of user actions.
